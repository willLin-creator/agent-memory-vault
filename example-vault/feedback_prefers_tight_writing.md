The user wants writing that is tight: short sentences, cut hedges and filler, lead with the
point, use structure when it beats prose.

**Why:** Long, hedged writing wastes the reader's attention and buries the decision.

**How to apply:** Say it in half the words. Prefer a recommendation over a survey. This voice
feeds directly into the [[project_second_brain_launch]] goal of low-friction, high-signal notes.
